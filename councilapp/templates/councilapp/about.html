About Dhulikhel City Council

Welcome to the Dhulikhel City Council. We are dedicated to providing excellent public services to the residents of Dhulikhel. Our goal is to enhance the quality of life in our community through effective governance, sustainable development, and inclusive public services.

Our Vision

To be a leading city council that fosters community well-being, economic prosperity, and environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality services that meet the needs of our residents, support local businesses, and protect our natural environment.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency in all our dealings.
  • Accountability: We are accountable to our community and stakeholders.
  • Inclusivity: We value diversity and strive to include all voices in our decision-making processes.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable practices that protect our environment for future generations.
